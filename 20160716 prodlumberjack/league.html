Join a League

How it works:

No experience necessary – your AXEpert axe coaches will teach you and be there with you every week. In no time you’ll be experiencing the joy of hitting bullseyes! Our axe throwing leagues run for 8 weeks, with the first 7 weeks being round-robin play, and it all culminates in seeded playoffs (and crowning a league champion!) the final week.

What it costs:

The 8 week league costs $120+tax, plus if it’s your first league, you will need to buy an axe from us.

How to register:

Fill out the form below and let us know that you are interested and which nights you’d be interested in playing. Once we get enough interest, we’ll let you know!
